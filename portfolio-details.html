Question Answering App

Question Answering App is an application in which an user will input a paragraph and ask questions from the content of the paragraph. The system will give answers to the questions from the given paragraph. For example, given the paragraph “Dhaka is the capital city of Bangladesh. Many people live in Dhaka.” and the question “Which is the capital city of Bangladesh?”, the answer would be “Dhaka”.
A question answering system will help finding information efficiently. Generally speaking, we use search engines to search for relevant documents when we look for some information on the Web. However, because they show us documents, we must read the documents and decide whether they contain the information we need. It’s a bother. From this need, we have decided to develop this application. This has huge implications in business as a tool to automate important tasks like customer support inquiries or common requests for information in a context.
